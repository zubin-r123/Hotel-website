Our house Rules



  1. Check in time 2:00 pm and Check out time 11:30 am

  2. As per Guest House Policy early check in and late check out will be charged extra

  3. If any room extension required, please check room availability and current rates. Pay and extend your room well in advance for hassle free stay.

  4. Every guest needs to produce a valid photo ID proof along with the address at time of check in

  5. No refund on cancellation of rooms for a any reasons.

  6. Outside Visitors are not allowed into guest rooms.

  7. Smoking in the rooms strictly prohibited.

  8. Consumption of outside food and liquor is strictly prohibited in rooms.

  9. Keep your room keys intact with the key tags. Loss of room key will be charged Rs. 1000/-

  10. Linen is changed every third day at the request of the Guest.

  11. Money, other valuables and your vehicles brought into the property is at the guest's sole risk.
    Management accepts no liability and shall not be responsible for any loss or damage there to and guest remains solely responsible for the safekeeping of any such items.

  12. Any damage to Guest house property will have to be compensated by the guest.

  13. Inverter back up is available for 2 lights and a Fan.

  14. It is not allowed to take explosives, weapons, inflammable materials or other dangerous chemicals into the Guest House.

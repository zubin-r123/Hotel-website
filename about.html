About Us


Welcome to Vento Sereno, your home away from home. Nestled in a prime location with breathtaking views, we are committed to providing you with the best hospitality experience.

We consist of a team of professionals striving to make your stay comfortable, memorable, and enjoyable. Whether you are here for business or leisure, we have carefully designed our services and amenities to meet your every need.




Located in Candolim, within less than 1 km of Candolim Beach and 2.4 km of Calangute Beach, VENTO SERENO provides accommodation with a garden and free WiFi throughout the property as well as free private parking for guests who drive. Featuring cozy rooms, this property also provides guests with private balconies. The property is non-smoking and is situated 2.6 km from Sinquerium Beach.




At the guest house, every room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, fridge, and towels. VENTO SERENO features certain rooms with garden views, and the rooms are equipped with a balcony. All units will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle.




Our guesthouse is a sanctuary of calm and soothing tranquility. Nestled amidst lush greenery and away from the hustle and bustle of the city, it offers a serene retreat for weary travelers. The gentle rustling of leaves and the melodious chirping of birds create a peaceful ambiance that envelops our guests upon arrival. Whether you're sipping a cup of tea on the veranda, or unwinding in your cozy room, our establishment exudes a sense of relaxation and comfort. Our attentive staff ensures that your stay is as tranquil as the natural surroundings, making it the ideal haven for those seeking respite from the demands of daily life.




We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility. Our green initiatives ensure that your stay has a minimal impact on the environment.

Thank you for choosing us. We look forward to hosting you and providing you with an exceptional experience.
